## Full Description
VitalsLog is the honest vitals tracker. No fake phone-based measurements — just accurate manual logging that you and your doctor can trust.

**Why VitalsLog?**
• Log blood pressure (systolic/diastolic), heart rate, weight, and blood sugar in under 10 seconds
• Large scroll wheel inputs designed for everyone — no tiny buttons
• AHA color-coded blood pressure categories (Normal → Crisis) so you instantly understand your readings
• Beautiful trend charts to spot patterns over 7 and 30 days
• History timeline with trend arrows showing if you're improving
• Crisis alert if your BP reading is dangerously high — with one-tap 911 access
• Dark mode for comfortable reading anytime
• Export your data as CSV

**Premium ($2.99 one-time — no subscription!):**
• Unlimited history (free = 30 days)
• PDF doctor reports — professional, printable summaries to share with your healthcare provider
• Medication tracking with chart correlations
• Family profiles — track vitals for your whole family
• Extended chart ranges (90 days, 1 year, all time)

**Built for accessibility:**
• 56px+ tap targets (28% larger than standard)
• 18pt minimum body text
• High contrast colors meeting WCAG AAA targets
• Full VoiceOver/TalkBack support

**Privacy first:**
• All data stored locally on your device
• No account required
• No data sent to servers

VitalsLog is NOT a medical device. Color-coded categories are based on American Heart Association guidelines for informational purposes only.
