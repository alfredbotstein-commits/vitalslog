# VitalsLog Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Summary
VitalsLog stores all your health data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or health information on external servers.

## What Data VitalsLog Stores
VitalsLog stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Blood pressure readings (systolic, diastolic, pulse)
- Heart rate readings
- Weight readings
- Blood sugar readings
- Optional notes you attach to readings
- Your name (if provided, for PDF reports)
- Medication information (Premium feature)
- App preferences (theme, units, reminders)

## Health Data Sensitivity
We recognize that vital signs and health metrics are **sensitive personal health information**. That's why:
- **All data is stored exclusively on your device** using local database storage (IndexedDB)
- **No data is transmitted** to Loopspur servers or any third party
- **No account is required** to use VitalsLog
- **No analytics or tracking** collects your health readings
- **No advertising SDKs** are included in the app

## Data You Control
- **Export:** You can export all your data as CSV at any time from Settings
- **Delete:** You can delete all data permanently from Settings > Delete All Data
- **PDF Reports:** Generated locally on your device. Sharing is initiated only by you.

## Third-Party Services
- **RevenueCat** (in-app purchase processing): Processes your Premium purchase. RevenueCat receives standard purchase transaction data (no health data). See [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/).
- **No other third-party services** receive any data from VitalsLog.

## Children's Privacy
VitalsLog does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+/Everyone and contains no objectionable content.

## Data Retention
- **Free tier:** Readings older than 30 days are automatically removed from local storage
- **Premium:** All readings are retained indefinitely on your device until you choose to delete them

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website.

## Contact
For privacy questions: support@loopspur.com

## Medical Disclaimer
VitalsLog is NOT a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The color-coded categories are based on American Heart Association guidelines and are for informational purposes only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
